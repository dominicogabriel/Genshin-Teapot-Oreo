The Red Dye's

So as we said before that we need materials to build our teapot (Housing), Dyes is one of materials we need and Red is one out of 3 Dyes we have so far in Genshin
this Red dye isnt really that hard and we have 3 materials that we need to collect to make this color.
Obtaining this Dye isnt really hard but you do need time to grind for the material either you gonna make it to Red dye right away or you wanna prepare it and use it later.

There's 3 types of material that you can use to making this dye

  • Sunsettia
  • Valberry
  • Carrot

To be honest, gaining this material isnt that hard to farm it but need time to grind and exploring the map.
For example, Sunsettia, it does grow on trees but not all trees are grow this fruit. Sometimes it's an Apple or most time it's nothing.
Thats why i really recommend you to look up at your map and explore while you farming those woods you looking for. It will come fast and handy while you do that
