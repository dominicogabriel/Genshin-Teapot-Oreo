A beautiful morning

"What a happy little mountain"
was the first thing i thought when i arrived at this place.
I see a Mansion, surrounded with walls.
And a Bamboo road leading my way
towards the gate. When i see someone with a smile, Welcoming me

A little Walk

As we get along, She guide me around The Mansion
Telling me stories and show me a one pretty Dog she called Tin.
We go back to the place where i entered the mansion as she said
"Hey, follow me to Zen Garden"

A walk in a peak

After a cup of tea, she take me to
walk near a peak of this mountain. Where from the far i can see a big
Shrine with a 2 Lion statue facing us. And i told myself,
I'm Glad to be here.
